cabbage casserole  ~     keeping cabbage whole  cut out and discard core and any discolored  outer leaves  beginning at top of head and working with  leaf at a  time  remove  outer leaves  rolling each one back on itself until it  can be loosened intact from head and reserve  cut remaining cabbage  head into  wedges and cut each wedge crosswise into  pieces   separate layers of cabbage pieces  peel parsnips and diagonally cut  into   inch thick slices     bring a large kettle of salted water to boil and cook reserved cabbage  leaves until just tender   to  minutes  with a slotted spoon  transfer cabbage leaves to a kitchen towel to drain  add cabbage  pieces to boiling water and cook until crisp tender  about  minutes   with slotted spoon transfer pieces to colander to drain and transfer  to a bowl  add potatoes to boiling water and cook just tender  about   minutes  with slotted spoon transfer potatoes to colander to drain  and transfer to another bowl  add parsnips to boiling water and cook  until just tender  about  minutes  drain parsnips in colander     in a  inch heavy skillet cook onion in  tablespoons butter over  moderately low heat  stirring  until softened  add cabbage pieces   salt  nutmeg  and pepper to taste and cook mixture over moderate  heat  stirring occasionally  until cabbage is tender  about   minutes  if any liquid remains in skillet  cook mixture over high  heat until liquid is evaporated  about  minute     if using robiola  cut into pieces  stir robiola or mascarpone into  cabbage mixture and cook over moderate heat  stirring until cheese is  melted     preheat oven to  degrees     butter a  quart ovenproof round serving dish  about    to  inches  across and    inches deep  and line with cabbage leaves  letting  leaves overhang  to  inches  arrange half of potatoes in serving  dish and season with salt and pepper  top potatoes in dish with half  of cabbage mixture and arrange half of parsnips over cabbage  repeat  layering with remaining potatoes  cabbage mixture  and parsnips   arranging last layer of parsnips decoratively around edge of  casserole and leaving cabbage exposed in center  dot parsnips with  remaining   tablespoon butter  fold cabbage leaves over parsnips  and cover tightly with a double layer of foil  casserole may be  prepared up to this point  day ahead and chilled  bring casserole to  room temperature before proceeding  bake casserole until heated  through and bubbling  about  hour     let casserole stand  minutes before serving     yield   servings        recipe by  cooking live  show
