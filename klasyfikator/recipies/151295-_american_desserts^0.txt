blueberry and pecan crunch pie pie   note  see the  basic pie dough  recipe which is included in this  collection     preheat the oven to  degrees  roll the pie dough out to the size  and shape of a  inch pie tin  lay the dough into the pan and prick  the bottom with the tines of a fork  place a sheet of waxed paper  over the pie dough and weigh it down with pie dough weights or clean  dry beans  and blind bake in the preheated oven for  minutes  remove  from the oven and cool thoroughly  combine the berries  sugar   cornstarch  spices  citrus juice and zest in a bowl and mix well   sprinkle the cake crumbs on the bottom of the prebaked pie crust   fill the pie shell with the berry mixture and bake at          degrees for  minutes  while the pie is baking  combine  the dry ingredients for the pecan crunch  cut the butter into little  cubes  add to the dry mixture and  with the palms of your hands  rub  together to form large nuggets as big as peanuts  no larger than  walnuts  after the pie has baked for  minutes  sprinkle the crunch  all over the top  reduce the oven heat to  degrees and bake an  additional  minutes  if the crunch begins to darken too quickly   cover with aluminum foil  cool the pie for at least  hours before  serving  this recipe yields one  inch pie     recipe source  michael s place with michael lomonaco from the tv food  network    show   ml a broadcast     downloaded from their  web site   http   www foodtv com    formatted for mastercook by joe comiskey  aka mr mad    jpmda prodigy com  or  mad squad prodigy net          recipe by  michael lomonaco       pies juice and grated zest of   grated fresh ginger root  unsalted butter      stick   all purpose flour  day old pound cake crumbs  huckleberries  granulated sugar  recipe basic pie dough    granulated sugar  cornstarch  ground nutmeg  ground cinnamon  juice and grated zest of   ground cinnamon  pecans chopped
