braised lamb shanks lamb   note  see the  emerils essence information  recipe which is  included in this collection     in a large stock pot or braising pot  add the olive oil  season the  lamb shanks with salt and pepper  season the flour with emerils  essence  dredge the lamb shanks in the seasoned flour  coating each  side completely  when the oil is hot  sear the shanks for  to   minutes on each side  or until very brown on all sides  remove the  shanks and set aside  add the onions to the pan and saute for   minutes  add the celery and carrots and continue to saute for   minute  season with salt and pepper  stir in the garlic  bay leaves   and thyme  cook for  minute  deglaze the pan with the red wine   scraping the bottom and sides to loosen the browned particles  add  the potatoes and stock  bring the liquid up to a boil and reduce to a  simmer  add the lamb shanks and continue to cook for about  to     hours  basting the shanks often  or until the sauce is stew like and  the meat starts to fall of the bone  season with salt and pepper  the  shanks can be served alone with crusty bread or over blue cheese  polenta  this recipe yields  servings     recipe source  emeril live with emeril lagasse from the tv food  network    show   em a broadcast     downloaded from their  web site   http   www foodtv com    formatted for mastercook by joe comiskey  aka mr mad    jpmda prodigy com  or  mad squad prodigy net          recipe by  emeril lagasse       potatoes black pepper freshly ground lamb stock or dark stock garlic chopped parsley  fresh thyme chopped olive oil  new potatoes quartered salt to taste lamb  bay leaves  red wine  flour  celery medium diced carrots medium diced onions medium diced
