belgian endive and ham au gratin pork trim the bottom of the endive heads and remove the outer leaves if  necessary  cook in boiling salted water with a squeeze of lemon juice  for about  minutes until just tender  drain well and wrap each head  of endive in a slice of ham  then put into the baking dish  prepare  the sauce while the endive is cooking  to make this  heat the butter  in a saucepan  remove from heat  stir in the flour  return to the  heat and cook for several minutes   remove from heat and blend in the  milk  stir over a low heat until thickened  add seasoning and  tbsp  of the endive stock  add the cheese to the sauce  pour over the ham  and endived  top with crumbs and cheese  brown under the broiler   then top with sliced tomatoes  to serve  as soon as cooked   this  also make a good hors doeuvre for   serves      origin  marguerite patten recipe cards shared by  sharon stevens    posted to mm recipes digest v   by pattm execpc com on apr    main dish   other head belgian endive  grated cheddar or gruyere  to   c fresh soft bread  flour  to   c grated cheddar or  milk  tomatoes  butter or margarine  seasoning  topping  thin slices cooked ham  squeeze lemon juice  sauce
