bourbon burnt sugar sauce  in a dry large deep heavy skillet cook the sugar over moderately high  heat  stirring constantly with a fork  until it is melted completely  and a deep golden caramel  remove the skillet form the heat  into the  side of the skillet carefully pour   cup hot water  a little at a  time  and cook the mixture over moderate heat  stirring  until the  caramel is dissolved  add the bourbon and simmer the sauce for   minutes  remove the skillet form the heat  let the sauce cool  slightly  and pour it into a heatproof pitcher  the sauce will  thicken as it cools  to return the sauce to a liquid state set the  pitcher in a saucepan of barely simmering water and stir the sauce  until it reaches the desired consistency  serve the sauce warm over  ice cream     makes about    cups     gourmet november            sauce sugar  bourbon
